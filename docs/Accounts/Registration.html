How to Register

On Tenderwell we offer multiple ways to create accounts. You can opt between using SSO solutions such as Google or Azure, DevelopmentAid or the Registration form.

Registration form​

The registration form is a simple form that allows you to create an account. There are 3 steps: Personal details, Company details and Password Creation.

Not all fields are required. However, providing them at registration will make it easier for us to manage your account later on.

Once you submit the form we will send you an email with a link to verify your account. Click on the provided link to verify your account.

Once verification is complete you will be able to log in and use your account. All new accounts come with a Free Plan which offers limited access to our services.

Sign up with Google​

Using the "Sign up with Google" button you can create your account using your Google account. You will not be required to set a password and your email will be automatically verified. We will require some additional details and then you will be able to log in and use your account.

Sign up with Microsoft​

Using the "Sign up with Microsoft" button you can create your account using your Microsoft account. Your email will be automatically considered verified and you will not be required to set a password.

Sign in with DevelopmentAid​

Using the "Sign in with DevelopmentAid" button you can sign in using your DevelopmentAid account. This option requires you to already have an account on DevelopmentAid.

Upon clicking the button you will be prompted for your DevelopmentAid username and password.

Your account will then be automatically created and verified based on your DevelopmentAid account details. If you have a DevelopmentAid subscription which includes Tenderwell access we will automatically provision your account for you. You will then be able to enjoy your upgraded subscription.
