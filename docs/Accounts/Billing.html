Billing

If you purchased a subscription to a paid plan directly from the Tenderwell website then you can view and download the payment invoices from the Billing page

You can access the Billing page through the Billing link in the User Profile menu

note

You have to be signed in to access the Billing page

Accessing the Billing page​

  1. Click on the profile icon in the top navigation bar
  1. In the menu that opens click on the Billing link

 

tip

You can also access the billing page by going directly to this link: Tenderwell Billing

Page overview​

On the billing page you can see the list of all of your invoices. Each invoice in the list has the following information associated with it:

  • Title - Invoice title, which contains the name of your Tenderwell plan
  • Date - Date when the invoice was created
  • Amount - Amount on the invoice
  • Status - Status of the invoice, can be one of:
    • Draft or Open - Invoice has been created and is awaiting the automatic payment
    • Paid - Invoice has been successfully paid
    • Void - Invoice has been cancelled
  • Download - Link to download the invoice as a PDF document
