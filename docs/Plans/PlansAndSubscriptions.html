Plans and Subscriptions

Visitors without an account have limited possibilities on Tenderwell. Once you create an account on Tenderwell you automatically are assigned a free plan.

Your plan will allow you limited access to our services. A list of the available services and usage limits are available on our plans page. These include among others:

  • Number of tenders/organizations you can open (per day).
  • Number of searches you can make (per day).
  • How many tender alerts you can create (global).
  • Team size (global).
  • Bulk tender export (export to Excel).

Some of these limits are per day others are global. Daily limits are reset every day at 00:00 UTC.

Free Plan​

The free plan is our default plan. It allows clients basic access to our services. For most clients these limits are enough to allow them to appreciate and make some basic use of our services.

Premium Plan​

The premium plan offers higher limits and is intended for business usage. You get higher limits but also the ability to work with a team. You can invite your colleagues to share your plan.

Custom plans​

For high usage or bulk clients we can create custom plans. Depending on your requirements we can package different limits and usage rates. Feel free to contact us for more details.

Free Trial​

If you are not sure about what plan is best for you we have a free trial available. A free trial offers you access to a plan for testing purposes. There are two ways to get a free trial: contact our sales department or take the free trial offer on our home page or any of the banners.

Free trials are processed with Stripe our payment processor. You provide your credit card but you will only be charged after your trial period ends. You can cancel any time.

Trials provided by our sales department do not require a credit card.
